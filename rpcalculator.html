Reward Points Calculator

How to Use the Reward Points Calculator

  1. Check the cards you have.
  2. Enter the amount you plan to spend (INR).
  3. Click 'Select All' to select all cards or manually select the desired cards (Atleast 2)
  4. Click 'Calculate' to see the results.
  5. De-select the card you want to exclude and Click 'Calculate' again to see the results.
